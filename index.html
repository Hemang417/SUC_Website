About Us

KNOW A BIT ABOUT OUR STORY

Established 27 years ago, the Student Union Council is the student body of Ramrao Adik Institute of Technology, Nerul. We are the organising committee behind all the cultural events that take place in the college like FE Orientation, Teacher’s Day, Vijay Patil Sir’s Birthday, Ganesh Utsav Celebration, Independence Day, Garba Celebration, Fresher’s Day, Republic Day, Traditional Day, Tie and saree day, BE Farewell and Horizon. Our committee is the voice of the students of the college and a source of an individual's overall development apart from the academics. We also overlook the anti-ragging committee and ensure the safety and comfort of the students, making their college journey, a smooth one.

Our Events

EXPERIENCE THE BEST EVENTS IN NAVI MUMBAI

RAIT-SUC organises the biggest cultural fest of Navi Mumbai-'Horizon', every year. The Horizon fest is an amalgam of fun, learning, high-spirits, adrenaline and unforgettable moments. With a buffet of day and night events, this fest offers a variety of flavours, a little of everything for all the different personalities out there. In this 3-day extravaganza, we have day events like paintball, mechanical bull, karaoke, flashmob, bungee run and many others which bring out our competitive spirits. We also have inter-college competitions like the footloose dance competition and then, we have night events like EDM night and Bollywood night which are the most awaited events and certainly the most enjoyable too.
